
Ingredients
1 Eggs
2 Cheese 
3 Butter

Tools
1 Fork
2 Frying pan
3 Plate
4 Stove

Instructions
1 Turn stove on low fire, then place frying pan on burner
2 place one 6th of a stick of butter in the frying pan
3 Melt butter and spread around pan
4 Crack two eggs and place in pan 
5 After 3 mins flip eggs
6 Turn off stove and place eggs on place
